Welcome to Raj Mesh Belt

Raj Mesh Belt CO. is one of the leading manufactures and suppliers of wire mesh belt in india and has been the first choice for our clients since 1990.
The company is leading the market through its research and development where we are effortlessly working for our customers to provide them with best quality products, services and support throughout. Meeting customer's requirements and their satisfaction is our priority and achievement.

Our Products

  • Chain Driven Belt

    Features a simplistic design, where successive spiral coils are interwoven to create an open mesh.

  • Gratex Belt

    Constructed by right and left hand spiral joined to produce stronger mesh ideal for carrying heavy loads.

  • Balanced Belt

    Economical and most widely used mesh belt in food processing unit, glass and ceramic industries. Highly durable and easy to install.

  • Enrober Belt

    Normally driven by toothed sprockets, made of (stainless) steel or plastic. Made from preshaped wires with the same form, weaving together.

  • Compound Balanced Belt

    Primarily suitable for unstable item needing very flat surfaces and considerable product support.

  • Honeycomb Belt

    A straight running belt with an extremely high strength to weight ratio, highly durable and economical.
