About Us

Welcome to Raj Mesh Belt

Raj Mesh Belt CO. is one of the leading manufactures and suppliers of wire mesh belt in india and has been the first choice for our clients since 1990. With more than 25 years in the belt manufacturing industry, our company has set the standard for manufacturing mesh belts at the highest levels of quality and value. The company is leading the market through its research and development where we are effortlessly working for our customers to provide them with best quality products, services and support throughout.Meeting customer's requirements and their satisfaction is our priority and achievement.Through inovation and developement our products have captured the market all over the country.We offer complete variety of metal belts which are widely used for different applications across various industries.
